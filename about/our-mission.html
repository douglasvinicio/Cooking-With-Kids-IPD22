What is the purpose of the site?

To Teach kids and teenagers how to cook in a fun and accessible way. Allowing them to discover easy-to-follow recipes in an easy-to-navigate website.

What are the objectives of the site?

To provide information about recipes while putting parents and kids to work together in a fun and educational together. Creating a bonding and healthy experience for the family while theyâ€™re learning how to cook a new dish.

What do we want the website to accomplish?

To give kids a comprehensive guide on cooking safely, meanwhile allowing advertisers to showcase their products (i.e. safe knives, cooking utensils aimed at kids and people learning for the first time).

What's the opportunity and problem the website addresses?

Opportunity: To encourage a healthier lifestyle for every member of the family, regardless of age. This is especially true for kids, who should be encouraged to start good eating habits as early as possible.
Problem: To show kids how fast-food and sugar affect them and provide healthy but tasty alternatives.
